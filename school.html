Workshop information

This workshop is 3 hours long held at high-school institutions in all metro areas Australia-wide. It is avaliable to any and all high school age students.

In the workshop you will focus on areas including;

  • who is apart of the LGBTI community
  • the importance of fostering mental health
  • how to recognise warning signs
  • how to start ‘the’ conversation
  • strategies to support struggling LGBTI individuals

All of our workshops are curated and run by fully-qualified professionals, with a minimum of 10 years experience in the field.
