Open workshops

Quick facts

Duration: 2.5 hours

Location: Level 4, 235 Macquarie St, Sydney, NSW 2000

Age requirements: 16 and over

Workshop information

This workshop is 2.5 hours long held at our Maquarie street base. It is avaliable to any individual 16 and over.

In the workshop you will focus on areas including;

  • the difference between mental illness and mental health
  • recognising warning signs
  • how to start ‘the’ conversation
  • strategies you can use to support struggling LGBTI individuals
  • suicide prevention

All of our workshops are curated and run by fully-qualified professionals, with a minimum of 10 years experience in the field.
